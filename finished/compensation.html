Comparisons with Similar Situations

The most important informatilon in negotiations involves comparisons with similar employment situations. The most persuasive comparisons begin with an explanation of why they are appropriate.

In choosing a comparator I take these things into consideration:

  • geographic location
  • size
  • type of employer
  • source of income
  • customer base

Although wages are of utmost importance, there are many other considerations. For example the costs of union requests for benefits or working conditions.

Without the help of professional with substantial education and experience to handle these details, expensive mistakes are likely.
