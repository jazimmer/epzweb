Economic Environment

Contracts are judged by two different groups of people: unions and management. Although these groups don't use the same criteria both need to understand the same economic environment.

That's where my education comes in—I can explain the current economic environment.

  • What are current and projected costs of living?
  • What are state and national norms for this type of work?
  • How will inflation and interest rates reflect the future of a contract?
  • How long do today's workers expect to stay in a job?

All these things will influence how each side of the negotiations interprets a proposed contract.
